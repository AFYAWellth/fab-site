About Us

Flowergirl and Birdie's Digital Art Space welcomes you to experience a fascinating intersection of human creativity and artificial intelligence. The journey into the world of Ai Art sparks a growing fascination with Ai Technology, and it's ability to transform traditional art forms. The innovative field of Ai Art has led us to discover a whole new realm of artistic possibilities that pull people together through the inspiration and creativity of talented artists from the various cultures of New Mexico.

To compliment the unique connection between the art and its collector, we plan to pair each piece with an official certificate of authenticity stating the name of the artist and the purchaser's name.
