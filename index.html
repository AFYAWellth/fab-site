About Us

  • Welcome to Flowergirl and Birdie's Digital Art Space
  • We are passionate novice Ai Artists with the dream of
  • curating an online gallery to showcase the fascinating
  • intersection of creativity and artificial intelligence.

  • Our journey into the world of Ai Art has been sparked
  • by the growing fascination with Ai Technology
  • and how it can transform traditional art forms.

  • The innovative field of Ai Art has led us to discover
  • a whole new realm of artistic possibilities that
  • we excited to share with you.


  • This novice portfolio focuses on the transformation of art using artificial intelligence.


  • The aim of the artist is to pull people together through the inspiration, creativity and the innovative thinking
  • we have developed though the various Hispanic cultures in New Mexico.



  • This portfolio is providing an avenue for a gallery to introduce individuals who are new to the Ai art trends.


  • Our target audience also includes gallery owners and art collectors.


  • The primary goal of this gallery is to showcase art and initiate contact with art gallery collections through LinkedIn.



  • The secondary goal of this site is to preserve the feeling of traditional cultural art.



  • We are looking at offering these artworks as premium, one-of-a-kind art pieces.



  • We are planning on presenting them for sale as museum-quality framed canvases soon.

  • To compliment the unique connection between the art and its collector,
  • We plan to pair each piece with an official certificate of authenticity
  • stating the name of the artist and the purchaser's name.
